SALVAGE SCHEME lifestyle blog

summary

With professional photographs of unrealistically picturesque life moments, outfits that were probably borrowed from the runway, and overly styled, probably inedible food, lifestyle blogs are largely devoid of content pertaining to the non-glamorous lifestyle. The Salvage Scheme attempts to eliminate this hole, giving readers ideas for infusing creativity and salvaged cool into their own daily existence with fewer monetary restrictions. Make good with what's already there.

branding & identity

The Salvage Scheme has a casual albeit classic identity. It provides non-fussy, sometimes oddly creative material to readers, and strives to instill creative bursts in whomever might be browsing the web that day, despite their supposed creative ability.

That being said, the Salvage Scheme's content is the focal point. With minimal design and a black and white color palette, a backdrop for colorful content is set. I used the Foodie Wordpress theme by Shay Bocks to acheive this design concept.

Photos are tinged with clean, cool filters or taken with similar lighting. Verbal content is humorous but substantial, with encouraging, sometimes comic honesty.

blog content

The Salvage Scheme's content takes the form of sartorial styling, home decor projects, impromptu cooking/recipe concoctions, and brief life essays. None of it is fancy, and almost all of it is repurposed (except for the writing, which is entirely original).
